About the Event

What is Epic Concert 2023?

Epic Concert 2025 is an annual music festival celebrating diverse genres and showcasing both established and emerging artists. Join us for an unforgettable experience filled with music, art, and community.

Venue

This year's event will take place in the beautiful Riverside Park, featuring multiple stages, food stalls, and art installations.
